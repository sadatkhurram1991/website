About Me


Assalamu Alaikum, I'm Ayesha Siddique, the heart behind Huroof by Ayesha. I’m a passionate young calligraphy artist from India, and I started this journey with a simple love for turning meaningful words into art. What began as a hobby is now a growing business where I create customized calligraphy frames in Arabic, Urdu, and English. Each piece is handmade with care and elegance. Whether it’s a gift for someone special or a spiritual piece for your wall, through Huroof by Ayesha, I hope to spread the beauty of calligraphy.
